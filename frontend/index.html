How it works
Up and running in 3 steps.
SlotWatch handles the constant monitoring so you don't have to keep checking the DMV website.
01
Create your account
Sign up with your email and set your preferences — zip code, radius, service type, and when you want appointments.
02
Set up notifications
Add your Pushover user key and email address. Download the free Pushover app on your phone to receive instant alerts.
03
Turn on monitoring
Flip the monitoring toggle on. SlotWatch checks for new slots automatically and alerts you the moment one opens up.
04
Book your slot
Tap the booking link in your notification and you're taken directly to the DMV page to confirm your appointment.
Notification setup
Getting Pushover
Pushover is a free app that delivers real push notifications to your phone — no phone number or carrier needed.
01
Download the app
Get Pushover from the App Store or Google Play. It's free to download and use.
02
Create an account
Sign up at pushover.net. Your User Key will be on your dashboard immediately after signup.
03
Add your key
Paste your Pushover User Key into the Notifications section of your SlotWatch dashboard.
